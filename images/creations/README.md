# My Creations - Image & Video Assets

This folder contains all the images and videos for your Minecraft Bedrock creations showcase.

## Folder Structure

Place your creation images and videos here following this naming convention:

### Custom Skins (8 items)
- `skin1.png` to `skin8.png`

### Custom Mobs (5 items)
- Images: `mob1.png` to `mob5.png`
- Videos: `mob1.mp4` to `mob5.mp4` (optional)

### Bedrock Addons (6 items)
- `addon1.png` to `addon6.png`

### Custom Maps (4 items)
- Images: `map1.png` to `map4.png`
- Videos: `map1.mp4` to `map4.mp4` (optional)

### Modeling & Texturing (7 items)
- `model1.png` to `model7.png`

### Setup Guides (3 items)
- `guide1.png` to `guide3.png`

## Image Guidelines

- **Recommended Size**: 800x600px or 16:9 aspect ratio
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: Keep under 2MB for faster loading

## Video Guidelines

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1280x720px (720p) or 1920x1080px (1080p)
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds recommended
- **File Size**: Keep under 10MB if possible
- **Note**: Videos will NOT auto-play. Users click to watch.

## How to Update Content

To update the creations data, edit the `creationsData` object in `/scripts/app.js`:

1. Change titles and descriptions
2. Update image/video paths
3. Add or remove items as needed
4. Modify category counts in `/index.html`

## Placeholder Images

If you don't have images yet, the system will show icon placeholders automatically.
